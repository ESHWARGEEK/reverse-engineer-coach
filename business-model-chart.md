# Business Model Chart: Reverse Engineer Coach Platform

## Executive Summary

**The Reverse Engineer Coach** is an AI-powered professional development platform that transforms how mid-to-senior level software engineers acquire production-grade architectural expertise. The platform operates as a **B2B2C learning infrastructure** serving both individual career advancement and enterprise talent development needs.
